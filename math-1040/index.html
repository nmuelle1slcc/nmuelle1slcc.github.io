📝 Signature Assignment

MATH-1040

Spring 2022 🌸

My signature project for MATH-1040 was to conduct multiple simulations and analyses using data about worldwide incidences and treatment successes of Tuberculosis. In this project, I used statistical techniques such as conducting hypothesis tests and calculating confidence intervals.

In my opinion, a critical part of the assignment was to ensure that my data met the conditions that are required to conduct tests and calculations. I believe that this step is crucial for statistics as a whole to ensure the fidelity of experiments and studies.
